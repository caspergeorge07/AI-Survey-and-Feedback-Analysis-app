## 1. Brand Positioning

SurveyIQ is an AI-powered survey and qualitative feedback intelligence platform for teams that need executive-ready insight from free-text responses.

## 2. Brand Promise

SurveyIQ helps organizations understand what people are saying, identify what matters most, and turn qualitative feedback into clear business action.

## 3. Voice and Tone

Voice:

- Clear.
- Confident.
- Professional.
- Evidence-led.
- Practical.

Tone:

- Calm for analysis.
- Direct for recommendations.
- Trustworthy for security and data handling.
- Executive-ready for reports.

## 4. Messaging Pillars

### From Feedback to Insight

SurveyIQ turns raw survey comments into themes, sentiment, risks, and recommendations.

### Built for Decision-Makers

Outputs are designed for management review, not just data processing.

### AI with Structure

SurveyIQ uses staged analysis, validation, and reporting workflows.

### Practical Exports

Users can download analysed CSVs and professional PDF reports.

## 5. Visual Direction

Starting direction:

- Clean SaaS interface.
- Business-focused layouts.
- Strong information hierarchy.
- Teal or blue-green primary accent.
- Restrained use of color for sentiment and status.

## 6. Product Language

Preferred terms:

- Analysis.
- Feedback responses.
- Themes.
- Sentiment.
- Confidence.
- Executive summary.
- Recommended actions.
- Management report.

Avoid:

- Overstated claims.
- Unexplained AI jargon.
- Casual wording in executive reports.

## 7. Do and Do Not

Do:

- Be specific about what SurveyIQ generates.
- Explain insights in business language.
- Make uncertainty clear when sample sizes are small.

Do not:

- Promise perfect accuracy.
- Imply AI replaces human judgment.
- Make enterprise claims before the product supports them.
