## 1. Wireframe Principles

- Prioritize clarity over decoration.
- Keep analysis workflows focused and linear.
- Make primary actions obvious.
- Support scanning, comparison, and repeated operational use.
- Avoid marketing-style layouts inside the authenticated app.
